    Hyperfocus

    One of the lesser-known symptoms of ADHD is "hyperfocus".

    If we find something that starts releasing dopamine in our brain, we become completely oblivious to anything else.

    You can see this in someone with ADHD who plays a video game for 7 hours and doesn't realise they need the toilet until they've nearly done something socially unacceptable.

    But once we learn how to harness it, it's incredibly powerful.

    For example, I wrote the dissertation for my Master's Degree in two nights. This is not a healthy approach, but did have 3 interesting outcomes:

    1. I got a Distinction
    2. It formed the basis of my book
    3. I am single-handedly responsible for that course now having a maximum word limit on dissertations
